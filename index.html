About Me

I am Ameer Hamza, a passionate Software Engineer and Educator based in Lahore, Pakistan. I hold a BS degree in Software Engineering from the University of Lahore (UOL), where I now serve as a Software Engineering Teacher — helping the next generation of developers grow their skills and think like professionals.


With over one year of hands-on professional experience, I specialize in Full-Stack Web Development — building everything from clean, responsive frontends using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and ReactJS, to powerful backends with NodeJS, ExpressJS, Django, and Python.


I have worked on real-world projects including a Transport Management System developed during my time at the International Islamic University, Islamabad, as well as various web development projects since 2020.


Beyond coding, I am deeply committed to education and mentorship. I believe in learning by doing — and I bring that same energy into my classroom. My goal is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry-ready skills for every student I teach.


Whether you are a student looking for guidance, a client with a web project, or a fellow developer wanting to collaborate — I am always open to connecting. Feel free to reach out!
